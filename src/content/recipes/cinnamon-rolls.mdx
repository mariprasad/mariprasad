Adapted from Sally's recipe, and a reliable weekend treat. Soft enriched dough, a cinnamon-sugar swirl, and enough cream-cheese frosting to be a little bit reckless.

## Dough

- All-purpose flour — 344 g
- Sugar — 50 g
- Salt — ½ tsp
- Milk — 180 ml
- Butter — 43 g
- Yeast — 7 g
- Egg — 1

## Method

1. **Warm the milk and butter** together to ~40 °C, then stir in the **yeast** and let it bloom.
2. Mix with the rest of the ingredients into a soft dough; knead until smooth.
3. Let it rise until doubled, roll out, spread with the cinnamon-sugar filling, roll up and cut into rolls.
4. Proof the rolls until puffy.
5. **Bake at 190 °C for 25 min**, until golden.
6. Cool slightly, then smother in cream-cheese frosting while still warm.
